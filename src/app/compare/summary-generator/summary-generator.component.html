Generate your own Summaries!

Leverage WikiRate's open data to generate dynamically comparison matrices based on your preferences. A comparison matrix is a table that summarizes various aspects of companies. Follow the steps to generate your summary:

  • Step 1.: Search Metrics by name and choose those that interest you! (with keydown you can browse to all metrics)
  • Step 2.: Select Year of Reference
  • Step 3.: Search for Companies and choose those that interest you! (with keydown you can browse to all companies)
  • Step 4.: Give a name to your summary!
  • Step 5.: Generate your Summary!
